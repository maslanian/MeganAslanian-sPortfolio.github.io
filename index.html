Understanding an Unknown Symptom of a Rare Brain Tumor

Schwannomatosis is a genetic disorder that results in tumor growth on the peripheral nerves. Although, it is very rare, the growth results in severe pain and neurological issues. The goal of this research involved understanding a correlation between thyroid dysfunction in a sample of patients with schwannomatosis. The results of the observational study can assist medical professionals in developing a treatment plan for patients' with this diagnosis. Additionally, providers can promote and refer interdepartmental collaborations for patients with similar features to the sample.
